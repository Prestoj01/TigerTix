Help Page

FAQs

What do I do if I forget my password?

Contact our help team using the email at the bottom of the page.

How are ticket prices and fees determined?

Prices are determined by the event coordinators, and prices of tickets being resold are determined by the seller.

How do I transfer tickets?

Go to your tickets in the homepage and click the transfer button, then input the person you would like to send it to.

How and when do I get paid for the tickets that I sell?

You can expect payouts from sold tickets within 3-5 business days. They are paid out via your verified bank account.

Any further questions please email to: tigertix@help.com
